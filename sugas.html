More About Us

Our mission at Sugas is simple. We define what some syndromes are and all the information a person needs
to know about it. We try to make this as easy to navigate as possible, while giving all of the necessary info
someone with (or knows someone else with) a syndrome has.

Sugas stands for:

S: Syndrome
U: Understanding
G: Genomic
A: Analysis
S: Solutions
